The pictures below serve to trace my decision to become a software engineer.

Note that this diagram is highly simplified.

1. I become increasingly conviced that learning to speak to computers will be the fundamental skill of our lifetime.
2. My job as a banker grows increasingly boring. The same tasks are repeated ad infinitum.
3. I see the movie Frozen. Elsa's musical number "Let it Go" serves as a powerful message to test my limits.
4. My interview with Greg Varias is enjoyable and successful; I'm admitted to Dev Bootcamp and decide to take the plunge into a new world.
